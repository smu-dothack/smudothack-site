## Who We Are

SMU .Hack is Singapore Management University's premier tech club. We're a community of builders, tinkerers, and learners passionate about technology.

## What We Do

- **Workshops** — Hands-on sessions on the latest tools, web development, AI/ML, and more
- **Hackathons** — Build projects, compete, and collaborate
- **Tech Talks** — Learn from industry professionals and fellow students
- **Project Teams** — Work on real-world projects with other members

## Our Mission

To foster a vibrant tech community at SMU where students can explore, learn, and build together — regardless of their major or experience level.
