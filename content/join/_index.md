## Why Join?

- **Learn** — Attend workshops and learn new tech skills
- **Build** — Work on projects with fellow members
- **Connect** — Network with like-minded tech enthusiasts
- **Compete** — Participate in hackathons and competitions

## Who Can Join?

All SMU students are welcome, regardless of major or experience level. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just getting started — there's a place for you here.

## How to Join

Join our Telegram channel to stay updated on our latest events and happenings!
