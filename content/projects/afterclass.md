.Hack members actively contribute to [Afterclass.io](https://www.afterclass.io), an open-source platform that helps university students make informed decisions about their courses through reviews and shared resources.

## How We Contribute

- Bug fixes and feature development
- Code reviews and testing
- UI/UX improvements

## Get Involved

Check out the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/afterclass-io/afterclass.io) to start contributing. Open issues are a great place to begin!
